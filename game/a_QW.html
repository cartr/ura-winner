Welcome!

Thank you for purchasing URA Winner, the only Undergraduate Readiness Assessment preparation software developed and recommended by the creators of the test.

You’re currently in the middle of a very important period of your life. In your high school classes, you’re learning skills you’ll need to successfully function as an adult. Outside your classes, you might be exploring hobbies, developing your personality, or making friendships that will last a lifetime. But don’t let these pastimes distract you from your most pressing responsibility: preparing for the Undergraduate Readiness Assessment.

The Assessment (also known as the URA) is used by colleges and universities around the world to evaluate their applicants in an unbiased, equitable manner. It covers both important academic skills and the “soft skills” and personality factors that have become increasingly sought-after by admissions counselors. It’s a difficult exam, and it holds the keys to your entire future. But there’s no need to be nervous; with URA Winner, you have all the resources you need to succeed.
