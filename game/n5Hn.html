The Theory and Practice of Etiquette

This wing of the school is pretty loud. Through the glass on the doors, the classrooms look pretty normal, and all the students are just sitting around having conversations with one another. You suppose that, even in a fancy school like this, talking to your friends is still probably one of the better ways to improve at socializing.

"This wing of the school," the instructor continues, "which we call the Conversation Wing, is where..."

You aren't sure you want to listen to this lady drone on for much longer.

"...studying theory is one thing, but it is in the arena of concrete practice where..."

She's probably already gotten through all the useful information.

"...how people learn to remove their masks and show their true, honest selves..."

Would she mind if you headed out now, you wonder?
