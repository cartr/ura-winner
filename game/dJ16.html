About This Website

URA Winner includes instructional content and example questions for each section of the Assessment. The example questions are similar in content, structure and difficulty to the ones you’ll see on test day, but the exact problems and texts shown will not appear on your test. Practice assessment results are for your information only and are not official judgments of the Assessment Committee.

To get the most of URA Winner, please be aware of the following features of your Web browser:

  • The “Back” button allows you to go back to pages you’ve previously visited. If you ever click a link you didn’t mean to, use the Back button.
  • The “Favorites” or “Bookmarks” menu or toolbar allows you to save the locations of resources so you can refer back to them later.
  • For students with visual impairments, your browser’s “Zoom” feature can be used to increase the size of text.
